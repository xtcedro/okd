📖 About OK Devs 📖

🌟 OK Devs is a community-driven initiative dedicated to leveraging technology to empower Oklahoma. 💡 Through collaboration, education, and innovation, we help bridge the gap between technology and real-world solutions.

🌍 Our Mission 🌍

Our mission is to transform Oklahoma into a thriving hub of innovation and technology. We empower developers, entrepreneurs, and tech enthusiasts by providing resources, mentorship, and opportunities to create meaningful change in their communities.

👨‍💻 Meet the Founder 👨‍💻

Pedro Dominguez

As the founder of OK Devs, Pedro Dominguez is a passionate technologist with a vision to make technology accessible for all. With extensive experience in software development, IT solutions, and digital transformation, Pedro is committed to fostering a tech-driven Oklahoma.
